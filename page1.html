ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Denise Chandler, and I'm an online math tutor.

I would like to say I always knew I was going to be a teacher, but I didn't. I just knew I enjoyed showing and explaining things to others, and I really enjoyed solving math problems. Eventually the two things I enjoyed doing directed me to teaching math, which I am happy to have had the opportunity to do for the past 10 years.
​
Recently I made a choice to change my approach for teaching students based on the fact that I felt I was able to help students more when I worked with them either in small groups or 1-on-1 after school. Some of my students would attend tutoring after school initially feeling discouraged because they "didn't get the lesson" or they "couldn't do the problems", but as we worked together without many of the distractions of the classroom, I could see their confidence build. 3-D Math Tutoring was created to provide students with additional learning support outside the confines and distractions of the classroom in order to help students believe they CAN DO math.
​
I enjoy teaching and tutoring math because I want to help the students I work with feel that math is doable. It is so incredible to see students' outlook about math change from "I can't do this" to "I can do this math!"
